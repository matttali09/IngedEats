About Us
“Can’t figure out what to eat? Snack it!”

Hungry? Ever had one of those “the fridge is full, but I don’t know what to eat!” moments? Snack Attack is here to help! Simply enter the ingredients you have at hand and Snack Attack will give you delicious recipes using those ingredients. Decided you don’t feel like cooking after all? Snack Attack will also give you restaurants in the area where you can get those same foods without all the work (and all the dishes!). And finally, for the brave, Snack Attack has a “Surprise Me” option where you can enter your location and find restaurants in the area with a wide variety of food options! Try something new today, with Snack Attack!
